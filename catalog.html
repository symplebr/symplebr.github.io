Systems Engineering Fundamentals

by Symple

Learning Objectives:

This course will provide the essential foundations of the Systems Discipline. We will open the doors to the Systems Engineering World by stimulating systemic thinking in a dynamic and interactive way, providing the basis for understanding advanced themes in the subject.

This knowledge is highly recommended for all of the following Symple training courses.

Content:

  • Systems Discipline History
  • Systems Theory
  • Systems Approach
  • Emergence
  • Lifecycle
  • Types of systems
  • Complexity
  • Tools to deal with complexity
  • Applied Systems Approach
  • Systems Engineering concept

  • Duration: 8h
  • Online Duration: 4h

Introduction to Systems Engineering

by Symple

Learning Objectives:

This course covers the processes that guide the applicability of Systems Engineering according to the International Council on Systems Engineering Council, INCOSE.

It also includes knowledge of engineering specialties and the tailoring process, which deals with the suitability of Systems Engineering processes for their context and their business, which can bring benefits not only for large companies and institutes, but also for medium and small organizations.

Content:

  • Lifecycle models
  • Tailoring process
  • SE technical processes
  • SE technical management processes
  • Agreement process
  • Organization-enabling process
  • Specialty engineering

  • Duration: 16h (2 X 8h)
  • Online Duration: 10h (2 X 5h)

Requirements Engineering

by Symple

Learning Objectives:

This course addresses important questions about requirements from their capture, analysis, specification, validation and verification as well as their management throughout the life cycle of a project.

A good requirements analysis that includes context analysis in its elaboration is fundamental for us to have good quality and completeness of the requirements set.

This course involves the student in a dynamic and interactive way with case studies and project challenges, approaching the practice of real life, showing the challenge and importance of the requirements activity in a company, school and project.

Content:

  • Why Requirements Engineering?
  • Needs elicitation
  • Requirements definition
  • Requirements types
  • Requirements analysis
  • Requirements specification
  • Technical measures
  • Requirements verification and validation
  • Requirements management

  • Duration: 32h (4 X 8h)
  • Online Duration: 16h (2 X 8h)

Systems Architecting Fundamentals

by Symple

Learning Objectives:

This course covers activities and practices in the development of architectural proposals for systems and solutions in order to meet the needs of customers.

The principles of how to architect systems are explored in a practical way, in addition to being based on the best worldwide references on the subject and counting on the state of the art in the experience our Symple specialists.

Content:

  • Definition of needs, operational concept and requirements
  • Functional analysis, decomposition and allocation
  • Systems modeling, systems analysis and trade-off studies
  • Allocation, traceability and requirements control
  • Prototyping, Integration and Verification
  • System Engineering and Process Control Results
  • Configuration and Data Management
  • Risk management approaches
  • Technical engineering reviews and their purposes

  • Duration: 32h (4 X 8h)
  • Online Duration: 16h (2 X 8h)

STPA Analysis

by Symple

Learning Objectives:

This course applies the Systems Approach to the systems emergent proporties, such as safety and security. The methodology on which the course is based is STAMP for the analysis of hazards associated with losses of value to stakeholders linked to properties emerging from the system.

STPA presents itself as a robust and comprehensive analysis in the initial phases of projects where there are many uncertainties and complexity associated with the factors for decision making.

It combines practice and dynamics, in addition to having the state of the art in the experience of our Symple specialists.

Content:

  • Systems Approach
  • Paradigm change
  • Architecting process
  • Systems Approach applied to Safety (loss of value / emergent properties of systems)
  • Techniques to apply STAMP: STPA Analysis
  • STPA related to Systems Engineering Processes
  • Best practices

  • Duration: 16h (2 X 8h)
  • Online Duration: 8h (2 X 4h)
