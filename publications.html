Systems Approach to the Design of Commercial Aircraft

By Scott Jackson and Ricardo Moraes

Systems Approach to the Design of Commercial Aircraft describes commercial aircraft from an advanced systems perspective, addressing complexity, cybersecurity and systems architecting.

It provides an explanation of Systems Engineering, describes how SE forms a framework for commercial aircraft, addresses how SE and systems architecting relate to commercial aircraft, addresses complexity and shows how humans fit into SE and the importance for commercial aircraft.

It shows how cybersecurity plays an important role in this combination and how human interface fits together.

  • Scott Jackson is a fellow of the International Council on Systems Engineering(INCOSE) and author of Systems Engineering for Commercial Aircraft (1997 and 2015) in English and Chinese.

  • Ricardo Moraes dos Santos is founder of Symple, Senior Systems Engineer at Embraer S / A and Vice President of INCOSE Brasil.
